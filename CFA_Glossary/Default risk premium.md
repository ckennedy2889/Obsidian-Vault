# Default risk premium

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

Compensation for the possibility that the issue of a debt instrument will fail to make a promised payment at the contracted time and in the contracted amount.

## Definition (Level I)

An extra return that compensates investors for the possibility that the borrower will fail to make a promised payment at the contracted time and in the contracted amount.
