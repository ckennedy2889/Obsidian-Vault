# Effective duration

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

Duration adjusted to account for embedded options.

## Definition (Level I)

The sensitivity of a bond’s price to a change in a benchmark yield curve.

## Definition (Level II)

Sensitivity of the bond’s price to a 100 bps parallel shift of the benchmark yield curve, assuming no change in the bond’s credit spread.
