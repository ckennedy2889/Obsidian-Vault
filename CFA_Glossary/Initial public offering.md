# Initial public offering

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

The initial issuance of common stock registered for public trading by a formerly private corporation.

## Definition (Level I)

(IPO) The first issuance of common shares to the public by a formerly private corporation.

## Definition (Level II)

(IPO) The initial issuance of common stock registered for public trading by a formerly private corporation.
