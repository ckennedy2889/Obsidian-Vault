# Option-adjusted spread

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level II)

(OAS) Constant spread that, when added to all the one-period forward rates on the interest rate tree, makes the arbitrage-free value of the bond equal to its market price.

## Definition (Level III)

The constant spread that, when added to all the one-period forward rates on the interest rate tree, makes the arbitrage-free value of the bond equal to its market price.

## Definition (Level I)

OAS = Z-spread – Option value (in basis points per year).
