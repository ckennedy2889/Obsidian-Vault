# Matrix pricing

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

An approach for estimating the prices of thinly traded securities based on the prices of securities with similar attributions, such as similar credit rating, maturity, or economic sector. Also called evaluated pricing.

## Definition (Level I)

Process of estimating the market discount rate and price of a bond based on the quoted or flat prices of more frequently traded comparable bonds.
