# Survivorship bias

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level II)

Bias that may result when failed or defunct companies are excluded from membership in a group.

## Definition (Level III)

Bias that arises in a data series when managers with poor track records exit the business and are dropped from the database whereas managers with good records remain; when a data series as of a given date reflects only entities that have survived to that date.

## Definition (Level I)

The bias resulting from a test design that fails to account for companies that have gone bankrupt, merged, or are otherwise no longer reported in a database.
