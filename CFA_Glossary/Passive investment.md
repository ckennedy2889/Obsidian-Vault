# Passive investment

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

Investment that seeks to mimic the prevailing characteristics of the overall investments available in terms of credit quality, type of borrower, maturity, and duration rather than express a specific market view.

## Definition (Level I)

A buy and hold approach in which an investor does not make portfolio changes based on short-term expectations of changing market or security performance.
