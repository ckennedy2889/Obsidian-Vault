# Yield to maturity

Annual return that an investor earns on a bond if the investor purchases the bond today and holds it until maturity, all promised payments are made in full and on time, and coupons are reinvested at the original yield. It is the [[Internal rate of return]] that equates the present value of the bond's expected cash flows with the bond's [[Full price]].

For the full Level II explanation, see [[Yield to Maturity]].
