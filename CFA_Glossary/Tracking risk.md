# Tracking risk

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level II)

The standard deviation of the differences between a portfolio’s returns and its benchmark’s returns; a synonym of active risk. Also called tracking error.

## Definition (Level III)

The standard deviation of the differences between a portfolio’s returns and its benchmark’s returns; a synonym of active risk. Also called tracking error.

## Definition (Level I)

The standard deviation of the differences between a portfolio’s returns and its benchmark’s returns; a synonym of active risk. Also called tracking error.
