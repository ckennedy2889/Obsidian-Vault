# Time value

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

The difference between the market price of an option and its intrinsic value, determined by the uncertainty of the underlying over the remaining life of the option.

## Definition (Level I)

The difference between the market price of the option and its intrinsic value, determined by the uncertainty of the underlying over the remaining life of the option.
