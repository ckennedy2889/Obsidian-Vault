# Bottom-up approach

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

A credit strategy approach that involves selecting the individual bonds or issuers that the investor views as having the best relative value from among a set of bonds or issuers with similar features.

## Definition (Level II)

With respect to forecasting, an approach that usually begins at the level of the individual company or a unit within the company.
