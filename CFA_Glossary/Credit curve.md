# Credit curve

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level I)

A curve showing the relationship between time to maturity and yield spread for an issuer with comparable bonds of various maturities outstanding, usually upward sloping.

## Definition (Level II)

The credit spreads for a range of maturities of a company’s debt; applies to non-government borrowers and incorporates credit risk into each rate.
