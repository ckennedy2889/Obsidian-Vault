# Indenture

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level I)

Legal contract that describes the form of a bond, the obligations of the issuer, and the rights of the bondholders. Also called the trust deed.

## Definition (Level II)

A written contract between a lender and borrower that specifies the terms of the loan, such as interest rate, interest payment schedule, or maturity.
