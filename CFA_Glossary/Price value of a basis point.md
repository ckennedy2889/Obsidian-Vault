# Price value of a basis point

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

(PVBP) The change in the bond price for a 1 basis point change in yield. Also called basis point value (BPV).

## Definition (Level I)

A version of money duration, it is an estimate of the change in the full price of a bond given a 1 basis point change in the yield-to-maturity.
