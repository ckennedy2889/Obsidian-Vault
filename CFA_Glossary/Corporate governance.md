# Corporate governance

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

The system of internal controls and procedures used to define and protect the rights and responsibilities of various stakeholders.

## Definition (Level I)

The system of internal controls and procedures by which individual companies are managed.

## Definition (Level II)

The system of principles, policies, procedures, and clearly defined responsibilities and accountabilities used by stakeholders to overcome the conflicts of interest inherent in the corporate form.
