# Callable bond

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level I)

A bond containing an embedded call option that gives the issuer the right to buy the bond back from the investor at specified prices on pre-determined dates.

## Definition (Level II)

Bond that includes an embedded call option that gives the issuer the right to redeem the bond issue prior to maturity, typically when interest rates have fallen or when the issuer’s credit quality has improved.
