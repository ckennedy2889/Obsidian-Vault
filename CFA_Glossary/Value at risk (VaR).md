# Value at risk (VaR)

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level II)

The minimum loss that would be expected a certain percentage of the time over a certain period of time given the assumed market conditions.

## Definition (Level III)

A probability-based measure of loss potential for a company, a fund, a portfolio, a transaction, or a strategy over a specified period of time.
