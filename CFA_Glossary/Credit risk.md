# Credit risk

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

The risk of loss caused by a counterparty’s or debtor’s failure to make a timely payment or by the change in value of a financial instrument based on changes in default risk. Also called default risk.

## Definition (Level I)

The risk of loss caused by a counterparty’s or debtor’s failure to make a promised payment. Also called default risk.

## Definition (Level II)

The risk that the borrower will not repay principal and interest. Also called default risk.
