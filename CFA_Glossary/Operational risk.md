# Operational risk

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level II)

The risk of loss from failures in a company’s systems and procedures, or from external events.

## Definition (Level III)

The risk of loss from failures in a company’s systems and procedures (for example, due to computer failures or human failures) or events completely outside of the control of organizations (which would include “acts of God” and terrorist actions).

## Definition (Level I)

The risk of loss from failures in a company’s systems and procedures.
