# Asset-backed securities

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level I)

A type of bond issued by a legal entity called a special purpose entity (SPE) on a collection of assets that the SPE owns. Also, securities backed by receivables and loans other than mortgages.

## Definition (Level II)

A type of bond issued by a legal entity called a special purpose vehicle (SPV), on a collection of assets that the SPV owns. Also, securities backed by receivables and loans other than mortgage loans.
