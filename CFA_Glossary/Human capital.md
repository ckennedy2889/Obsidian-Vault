# Human capital

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level III)

An implied asset; the net present value of an investor’s future expected labor income weighted by the probability of surviving to each future age. Also called net employment capital.

## Definition (Level I)

The accumulated knowledge and skill that workers acquire from education, training, or life experience and the corresponding present value of future earnings to be generated by said skilled individual.

## Definition (Level II)

The accumulated knowledge and skill that workers acquire from education, training, or life experience.
