# Convertible bond

**Program:** CFA® Program

## Definition (Level I)

Bond that gives the bondholder the right to exchange the bond for a specified number of common shares in the issuing company.

## Definition (Level II)

Bond with an embedded conversion option that gives the bondholder the right to convert their bonds into the issuer’s common stock during a pre-determined period at a pre-determined price.
