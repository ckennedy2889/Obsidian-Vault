## Text Elements
Which Valuation Model to Use? ^t_txt

DDM
(Dividend Discount) ^h0_t

FCF Approach
(FCFF or FCFE) ^h1_t

Residual Income ^h2_t

Use when company: ^rl_t

Pays stable, predictable
dividends tracking earnings ^r190_0_t

Doesn't pay dividends OR
dividends differ from FCFE ^r190_1_t

Doesn't pay dividends AND
has negative free cash flow ^r190_2_t

Minority shareholder
(can't control div policy) ^r280_0_t

Controlling shareholder
(can redirect all FCF) ^r280_1_t

Has high-quality,
transparent financials ^r280_2_t

Dividends clearly reflect
earning power ^r365_0_t

FCF better captures
true cash generation ^r365_1_t

Book value is meaningful
(banks, early-stage firms) ^r365_2_t

Key Formula:                        V₀ = Σ Dₜ/(1+r)ᵗ                          V₀ = Σ FCFEₜ/(1+r)ᵗ                       V₀ = B₀ + Σ RIₜ/(1+r)ᵗ ^frml_t

Relative Valuation (Multiples: P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA) = always used alongside, not instead of, a fundamental approach ^note_t
